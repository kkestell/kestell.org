# Tiger's Milk

Adele Davis' original recipe.

## Ingredients

* 1 cup milk
* 1⁄4 cup powdered milk
* 1⁄3 of a banana
* 1 tablespoon brewer's yeast
* 2 tablespoons wheat germ

## Instructions

1. Blend all ingredients together.
2. Drink immediately.
