### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient          |
|--------|---------------------|
| 1 box  | Jiffy Cornbread Mix |
| 2 ea   | Eggs                |
| 1⁄2 cup  | Sour Cream          |
| 1⁄4 cup  | Vegetable Oil       |
| 1 tbsp | Granulated Sugar    |
| 1⁄2 tsp  | Salt                |

### Notes

Bake in a 9" pan at 350°F for about 30 minutes.
